Sonic Speed

Simple Fluid

EoS Approach

According to Landau and Lifshitz (1959), sonic speed could be expressed as \[ u_s = V \sqrt{ -\frac{C_p}{C_v} \frac1{M_w} \left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial V}\right)_T } \] where $M_w$ is the molar weight of the fluid.

Pedersen and Christensen (2006) provide similar expression \[ u_s = \frac{V}{\sqrt{M_w}} \sqrt{\frac{C_p}{C_v} \left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial T}\right)_V \left(\frac{\partial T}{\partial V}\right)_P} \]
